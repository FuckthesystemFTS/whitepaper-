Token Distribution and Allocation

The total supply of FTS tokens is 100 billion, with a maximum burn of 50% of the total supply to ensure scarcity.

  • Airdrop and Airburn Phase (30%): For every token distributed during the airdrop, one token is burned, ensuring deflation and community engagement.
  • Play to Win and Play for Burn (20%): Gamified distribution incentivizes user participation, with one token burned for every two tokens distributed.
  • Remaining 50% of Supply:
    • 5% to the CEO's Project
    • 5% to the FTS Team
    • 10% for Project Development
    • 10% to the BRI Community
    • 10% for Marketing and Investments
    • 10% for Future Projects
